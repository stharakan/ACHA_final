Recall that the incoherence of two matrices $\Phi$ and $\Psi$ is given by 

\begin{align}
	\mu = \max_{i,j} |(\Phi^* \Psi)_{ij}|
	\label{incoherence}
\end{align}

We looked into the incoherence of the Hadamard basis with
\begin{itemize}
	\item Standard basis 
	\item Fourier basis
\end{itemize}
