$$
T = \begin{bmatrix}
	t_n & t_{n-1} & ...& t_1           \\[0.3em]

	\ddots &\ddots & \ddots    \\[0.3em]
	t_{2n-1}&t_{2n-2}& ... &t_n         
\end{bmatrix}
$$
